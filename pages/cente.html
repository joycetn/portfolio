Team: Individual Project

Role: Art Direction, Content Research, Branding, Print Design

Overview

Over the duration of three months, we were given the brief to construct a brand identity and target readership for our own unique magazine brand. The final product called for fully designed samples of our printed publication - templates for monthly designs, feature spreads, and page content layout.
