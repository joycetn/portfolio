Hi, I'm Joycelyn Tng (she/her), a UX design and print publishing student currently living on the unceded territory of the kʷikʷəƛ̓əm First Nation. I value creating whimsical designs that stem from inclusive design processes!

I believe that everyone has a value and important perspectives to consider when it comes to making design decisions.
